How to choose

  1. Read the paragraph, not the headline. The headline is Fraunces in all of them — the small text is the decision.
  2. Look at the button and the label. Those two bits of text appear hundreds of times across the site.
  3. Ask: does this look like a business that charges properly? That's the whole job of the body font.
