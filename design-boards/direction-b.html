The Night Press — system

Premium as gravitas: white ink and foil on black uncoated is a real product this shop can upsell, so a dark site doubles as a product demonstration. Terracotta #B84A26 is deliberately removed here — it measures 3.04:1 on ink and cannot legally carry a link on this field. Copper is used as foil, never as paint.

Palette & measured contrast

Field#171310 Deep warm black. Base of every dark act. Cream on it: 15.45:1 AAA.

Field 2#221C16 Raised surfaces, form fields. Cream on it: 14.10:1 AAA.

Cream#F2EADB All text on dark; ground of the light acts. Field on cream: 15.45:1 AAA.

Muted#B8AC98 Captions, spec lines. On field: 8.26:1 AAA.

Copper (foil)#E0A05A Rules, keylines, numerals. On field: 8.22:1 AAA. Field text on copper: 8.2:1.

Copper ink#8A4E18 Links + focus text on cream acts. On cream: 5.52:1 AA any size.

Excluded — terracotta#B84A26 3.04:1 on field. Fails AA. Not part of this direction.
