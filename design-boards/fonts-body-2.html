Body font — round 2  ·  Four genuinely different approaches, not ten versions of one

Round one was eleven versions of the same idea.

They were all neutral sans-serifs. To me that's eleven options; to anyone else it's one option shown eleven times — so of course none of them landed. My mistake.

These are four different approaches. The headline stays Fraunces throughout. Don't look for a favourite font — look for the group that feels right, then we refine inside it.

Don't pick a font — pick a group

Approach 1 = one font, maximum cohesion. Approach 2 = magazine, most expensive-feeling. Approach 3 = modern with personality. Approach 4 = technical print-shop language.

Tell me which approach feels closest, even if no single sample is perfect — I'll refine inside it. And if none of the four feel right, say that too: it means the problem isn't the font, and I should stop asking you about fonts.
