# Website Questions for the Print Shop
*Answer as many as you can — short answers are fine. Voice notes work too.*

## Part 1 — The Basics (must-have)
These go directly on the website, so exact spelling matters.
1. **Business name** — exactly as you want it written everywhere.
2. **Do you have a logo?** If yes, send the file (any format). If no, do you want us to design a simple one?
3. **Phone number** for the website — the one you want customers calling.
4. **WhatsApp number** — same as above, or different? (This will be the main "message us" button.)
5. **Email address** for inquiries. If you don't have a business email, we'll create one.
6. **Shop address** — full address, exactly as it appears on Google Maps.
7. **Business hours** — for each day, including weekends. Closed days too.
8. **How long have you been in business?** (e.g., "since 2010" — this builds trust on the site.)
9. **Any social media pages?** Instagram, Facebook, TikTok — send links if they exist.
10. **Do you already have a Google listing?** (When someone searches the shop name on Google, does it show up on the map with hours and reviews?)

## Part 2 — Services & Products (must-have)
11. **What do you print?** Go through this list and say yes/no — and add anything missing:
    - Business cards · flyers · brochures · postcards
    - Vinyl banners · roll-up banner stands · lawn signs / coroplast signs
    - Storefront signs · posters · foam boards
    - Stickers · labels · window decals · vehicle magnets/decals
    - T-shirts · hoodies · uniforms · embroidery · mugs
    - Wedding / shaadi cards · event invitations
    - Letterhead · envelopes · invoice books (NCR) · stamps
    - Design work (making the artwork for customers)
12. **What are your TOP 3 money-makers?** These go front and centre on the site.
13. **What do you want MORE of?** The website can push it.
14. **Anything you DON'T do?** So we never advertise something you can't deliver.
15. **Turnaround time** — typical order? Same-day or rush service?
16. **Minimum orders?** (e.g., can someone order just 1 t-shirt or 100 business cards?)
17. **Pickup only, or do you deliver?** If delivery — how far, and is there a charge?
18. **Service area** — Brampton only, or also Mississauga / Toronto / anywhere in the GTA?

## Part 3 — Pricing (must-have for the Rates page)
19. **Do you have a rate list?** Even a rough one — photo of a handwritten sheet is fine.
20. If not, give **starting prices** for common items, e.g.:
    - 500 business cards = $____ · 100 flyers = $____ · 3×6 ft vinyl banner = $____ · 1 lawn sign = $____ · 1 printed t-shirt = $____
21. **Are you okay showing price ranges on the website?** (Recommended — almost no competitor in Brampton does it. We show "from $X", never exact prices.)
22. **How do customers pay?** Cash, e-Transfer, card, other?

## Part 4 — Your Story & Customers (makes the site feel real)
23. **How did the business start?** Two or three sentences — when, why, who runs it.
24. **Who are your main customers?** Small businesses? Real estate agents? Restaurants? Walk-in families? Wedding customers?
25. **Why do customers choose you** over Staples or other print shops? (Price? Speed? Quality? Personal service? Be honest — this becomes the main message of the site.)
26. **What languages do you serve customers in?** (English, Punjabi, Urdu, Hindi, others — worth advertising in Brampton.)
27. **Any big or well-known customers** we can mention? (Only with their permission.)

## Part 5 — Photos & Proof (send whenever ready)
28. **Photos of your best work** — 10 to 20 photos of finished jobs. Phone photos are fine if sharp and well-lit.
29. **A photo of the shop** — outside (storefront) and inside (machines running = trust).
30. **A photo of you/the team** — optional but powerful for a family business.
31. **Happy customers** — any 2–3 regulars who'd write a one-line review or let us quote them?

## Part 6 — Small Decisions (needed before launch)
32. **Website address** — any preference? (e.g., shopname.ca — costs about $20/year, the only cost.)
33. **Who answers the WhatsApp and quote requests?** Whoever it is should expect messages once the site is live.
34. **Anything you've seen on another website** — any site you like, or anything you definitely DON'T want?

*Parts 1–3 are enough to finish and launch the website. Parts 4–6 make it great.*
